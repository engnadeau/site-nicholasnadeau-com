[AI Salon Montreal](https://www.aisalon.ai/montreal) partnered with [Amiral Ventures](https://www.amiral.vc/rising-tides/podcasts/ai-salon-x-flagship-live-mila) for a live podcast recording at [Mila](https://mila.quebec/), bringing together founders, investors, and builders to talk about what it actually takes to scale AI startups from Montreal to the world.

## Founders Panel

I moderated a conversation with three founders who've done it:

- **Alexis Steinman**, Co-CEO at [Maxa](https://www.maxa.ai/)
- **Mohannad El-Barachi**, CEO at [Wrk](https://www.wrk.com/)
- **Sylvain Perron**, CEO at [Botpress](https://botpress.com/)

## Full Lineup

- **Opening:** Laurent Charlin (Mila)
- **VC Panel:** Dominic Becotte (Amiral Ventures), Hugues Lalancette (Inovia Capital), Catherine Ouellet-Dupuis (WhiteStar Capital), moderated by Éléonore Jarry (Brightspark)
- **Keynote:** Sam Ramadori, moderated by Fred Bastien (Amiral Ventures)

The wins, the pivots, and the realities behind growth in AI. All in one room.
