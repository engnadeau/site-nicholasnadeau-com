Interius Farms is revolutionizing vertical farming by reimagining its design with multi-sided rotating towers that maximize spatial and cubic efficiency, making fresh, sustainable produce more affordable for Canadians.

<YouTube id="WbN6ZA3mPvw" />

In collaboration with Osedea, they are integrating advanced agtech solutions with robotics, specifically Boston Dynamics' Spot robot, to enhance automation and efficiency in controlled environment agriculture. This partnership showcases how robotics and AI-driven insights can optimize farm operations, reduce costs, and pave the way for the future of high-tech, scalable food production. ​​
